* **Fruits and vegetables.** Fresh fruits and vegetables are packed with nutrients and antioxidants, which are essential for good health. Aim to eat at least five servings of fruits and vegetables each day.
* **Whole grains.** Whole grains are a good source of fiber, which can help you feel full and satisfied. They also contain vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Aim to eat at least three servings of whole grains each day.
* **Lean protein.** Lean protein is essential for building and repairing tissues. It can also help you feel full and satisfied. Aim to eat at least one serving of lean protein each day.
* **Low-fat dairy products.** Low-fat dairy products are a good source of calcium, protein, and vitamin D. Aim to eat at least one serving of low-fat dairy each day.
* **Healthy fats.** Healthy fats are essential for good health. They can help to lower cholesterol levels, reduce inflammation, and improve brain function. Aim to eat at least one serving of healthy fats each day.

Here are some specific examples of foods that you can eat off:

* **Fruits:** apples, bananas, berries, grapes, oranges, pears, watermelon
* **Vegetables:** broccoli, carrots, celery, cucumbers, leafy greens, onions, peppers, tomatoes
* **Whole grains:** brown rice, oatmeal, quinoa, whole-wheat bread, whole-wheat pasta
* **Lean protein:** chicken, fish, beans, lentils, tofu
* **Low-fat dairy products:** milk, yogurt, cheese
* **Healthy fats:** avocados, nuts, seeds, olive oil
